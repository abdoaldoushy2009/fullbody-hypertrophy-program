Progression Model (Double Progression)

W1–2
Learn the movements
  • Start light — technique first, always
  • Hit the bottom of every rep range (e.g. 8 reps)
  • Focus on mind-muscle connection
W3–4
Add reps first
  • Push toward top of rep range (e.g. 10 reps)
  • Keep same weight until you hit top range on all sets
  • Last set should be RPE 8–9 (2 reps left in tank)
W5+
Add weight, reset reps
  • Add 2.5–5 kg to upper body, 5 kg to lower
  • Drop back to bottom of rep range
  • Repeat the cycle — this is linear overload
W9+
Deload if needed
  • Every 8–12 weeks: reduce weight 40–50% for 1 week
  • Protects joints and resets CNS fatigue
  • You'll come back stronger — not weaker
